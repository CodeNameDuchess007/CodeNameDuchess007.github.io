About Me

I'm a cybersecurity practitioner who thrives on learning, adapting, and building smarter solutions. Whether it’s digging into the latest 0-day exploits or figuring out the most cost-effective MFA setups, I stay curious and committed to staying ahead in this fast-moving field.

I earned my cybersecurity foundation from the University of San Diego, and I've kept pushing myself through hands-on labs, personal projects, and building custom environments across Linux and Windows VMs. Along the way, I picked up real-world experience setting up and securing AWS and Azure cloud environments.

Over time, I’ve found myself drawn deeper into data analytics and automation — areas where I can make an immediate impact. I've grown highly skilled in Power BI, especially in building complex reports using DAX scripting to pull out deeper, smarter insights.

On the automation side, I’ve expanded into advanced Power Automate development, using Workflow Definition Language (WDL) to create customized workflows that go way beyond basic templates — solving real business problems and saving teams serious time.

I love blending cybersecurity awareness, cloud experience, and a strong data-driven mindset to create solutions that are not only secure, but genuinely useful.
